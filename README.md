# 🏥 Healthcare EHR Requirements Analysis

> **Business Analyst Portfolio Project** | Epic Systems Implementation | HIPAA Compliant
>
> ---
>
> ## 📋 Project Overview
>
> End-to-end **Business Requirements Analysis** for a large-scale **Epic EHR implementation** at a regional healthcare network with 5 hospitals and 30+ outpatient clinics.
>
> **Role:** Lead Business Analyst | **Duration:** 8 months | **Tools:** Epic, JIRA, Visio, Confluence
>
> ---
>
> ## 🎯 Objectives
>
> - Gather and document business requirements from 20+ clinical and administrative stakeholders
> - - Model current-state and future-state workflows using BPMN 2.0
>   - - Develop User Stories with Acceptance Criteria (Gherkin format)
>     - - Coordinate UAT across 4 departments
>       - - Ensure full HIPAA compliance throughout all process flows
>        
>         - ---
>
> ## 📁 Key Deliverables
>
> ### 1. Business Requirements Document (BRD)
> - 150+ functional & non-functional requirements (MoSCoW prioritized)
> - - Full traceability matrix linking requirements to test cases
>   - - Epic modules covered: ADT, Orders, Billing, Ambulatory
>    
>     - ### 2. User Stories & Acceptance Criteria
>     - - 80+ user stories for clinical, administrative, and IT personas
>       - - Given/When/Then acceptance criteria format
>        
>         - ### 3. BPMN Process Models
>         - - 12 current-state swim-lane diagrams
>           - - Future-state workflows reducing manual steps by 35%
>            
>             - ### 4. UAT Test Cases
>             - - 200+ test cases (Happy Path, Negative, Edge scenarios)
>               - - Defect tracking logs and final sign-off documentation
>
